About Me

I am a UX designer with a Bachelor's degree in HCI/UX and a professional certificate in UX Design, and I bring a user-centered approach to crafting intuitive and engaging digital experiences. My background at Cummings Creative Group (CCG) has allowed me to hone my skills in user research, interaction design, and prototyping, collaborating with cross-functional teams to translate user needs into effective design solutions. I am adept at all stages of the design process, from wireframing and prototyping to usability testing and stakeholder presentations. My ability to synthesize information, solve problems creatively, and champion accessibility ensures designs are not only aesthetically pleasing but also highly functional and inclusive.
